Dopamine Tracker

Track your brain's stimulation patterns

Dopamine Tracker is an iOS app that helps you understand and monitor your daily dopamine-stimulating habits. Log your activities, see your daily score, and discover patterns over time — all privately on your device.

Features

  • Habit Tracking — Log dopamine-stimulating activities throughout your day with a simple, intuitive interface
  • Daily Dopamine Score — Get a clear daily score that reflects your stimulation levels at a glance
  • Streaks & Insights — Build healthy streaks and unlock insights about your behavioral patterns
  • Weekly Trend Charts — Visualize your dopamine trends over time with beautiful weekly charts

Subscription

Dopamine Tracker offers a weekly auto-renewable subscription with a 3-day free trial. Unlock all features and start tracking your patterns today.

Privacy First

All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Dopamine Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Your tracking data never leaves your iPhone.
