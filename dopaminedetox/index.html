Dopamine Detox

Give your brain the reset it needs

Dopamine Detox is an iOS app that guides you through structured digital and dopamine detox sessions. Take control of your stimulation habits with guided timers, structured programs, and motivational support — all privately on your device.

Features

  • Guided Detox Timer — Stay focused with a beautiful, distraction-free countdown timer for your detox sessions
  • Structured Programs — Choose from 7-day, 14-day, or 30-day detox programs tailored to your goals
  • Motivational Guidance — Receive encouragement and tips throughout your detox journey
  • Session Tracking — Track your completed sessions and build momentum over time

Subscription

Dopamine Detox offers a weekly auto-renewable subscription with a 3-day free trial. Unlock all programs and features to start your reset today.

Privacy First

All data is stored locally on your device. Dopamine Detox does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Your detox journey stays completely private.
