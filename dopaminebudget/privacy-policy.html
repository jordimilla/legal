Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Dopamine Budget ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Dopamine Budget does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All activity logs, budgets, and analytics remain exclusively on your device.

Local Storage

All your dopamine budgets, activity data, and spending analytics are stored locally on your device. Your data is never uploaded to any cloud service or server. You can delete your data at any time from within the app.

Device Permissions

Dopamine Budget does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, or any other sensitive device features. The app operates entirely without special permissions.

Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Analytics

Dopamine Budget does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Dopamine Budget does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
