Dopamine Budget

Budget your daily dopamine wisely

Dopamine Budget is an iOS app that helps you manage your daily stimulation like a budget. Allocate dopamine points to activities, track your spending throughout the day, and stay within your limits — all privately on your device.

Features

  • Daily Dopamine Points — Start each day with a set budget of dopamine points to spend on activities
  • Visual Gauge — See your remaining dopamine budget at a glance with a beautiful visual gauge
  • Activity Tracking — Log activities and their dopamine cost to stay mindful of your consumption
  • Spending Analytics — Review your dopamine spending patterns with detailed analytics and charts

Subscription

Dopamine Budget offers a weekly auto-renewable subscription with a 3-day free trial. Unlock all features and start budgeting your dopamine today.

Privacy First

All data is stored locally on your device. Dopamine Budget does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Your activity data and budgets never leave your iPhone.
