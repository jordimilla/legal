Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

At TraderCalc, we take your privacy very seriously. This policy describes what information the app accesses, how it is used, and what control you have over it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. TraderCalc is a standalone calculator application that performs all calculations locally on your device. No personal information is requested, collected, or stored.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

TraderCalc does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use advertising or ad networks
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not connect to any external servers or APIs

3. Local Processing

All calculations are performed exclusively on your device. TraderCalc has no backend server. The values you enter (prices, quantities, percentages) exist only in your device's memory during use and are not persisted or transmitted anywhere.

4. No Account Required

TraderCalc does not require user registration or sign-in. There are no accounts, no passwords, and no personal information collected at any point.

5. Permissions

TraderCalc does not request any device permissions. The app does not access your photos, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device feature.

6. Offline Operation

The app works entirely offline. No internet connection is required or used. All functionality is available without network access.

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted outside your device and no data is stored, the risk of data leakage is effectively zero. All information remains protected by iOS security measures (device encryption, sandboxing, etc.).

8. Children's Privacy

TraderCalc does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. However, the app is designed for traders and is not intended for use by children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
