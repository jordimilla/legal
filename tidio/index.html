Tidio

Smart phone cleanup for iPhone

Tidio is an iOS app that helps you free up storage on your iPhone by identifying and removing clutter. It analyzes your photo library, contacts, and videos to find items you can safely clean up.

Features

  • Photo Cleanup — Find and remove duplicate photos, similar shots, screenshots, blurry images, and large files
  • Contact Cleanup — Detect and merge duplicate contacts, find incomplete contact entries
  • Video Compression — Compress large videos to save storage space
  • Email Cleanup — Connect your Gmail account to categorize and bulk-delete unwanted emails (promotions, social, newsletters)

Privacy First

All analysis is performed locally on your device. Tidio does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Your photos, contacts, and emails never leave your iPhone.
