Features

  • Daily Dopamine Score — See how your daily habits affect your brain's stimulation levels with a clear, easy-to-read score.
  • Activity Tracking with Point System — Log activities throughout your day and earn or spend dopamine points based on their impact.
  • Guided Detox Timer & Programs — Follow structured detox sessions and programs to give your brain the reset it needs.
  • Insights & Analytics — Discover trends and patterns in your dopamine habits with detailed charts and weekly summaries.

Subscription

Dopamine offers a weekly and annual auto-renewable subscription with a 3-day free trial. The subscription unlocks all premium features. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account after the trial ends. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings.

Privacy First

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Your dopamine journey stays entirely private.
