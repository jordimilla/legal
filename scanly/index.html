ScanlyPDF

Smart document scanner for iPhone

ScanlyPDF is an iOS app that turns your iPhone into a powerful document scanner. Scan any physical document into a high-quality PDF, extract text with OCR, and organize your documents — all on your device.

Features

  • Document Scanning — Use your camera to scan documents into crisp, high-quality PDFs
  • OCR Text Extraction — Extract text from scanned documents to copy, search, and share
  • Document Management — Organize, rename, and share your scanned PDFs
  • One-Time Purchase — Unlock unlimited scans and OCR with a single lifetime payment

Privacy First

All scanning and text extraction is performed locally on your device. ScanlyPDF does not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Your documents never leave your iPhone.
