Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

ScanlyPDF ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

ScanlyPDF does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All documents, scans, and extracted text remain exclusively on your device.

Camera Access

ScanlyPDF requests camera access solely to scan physical documents. Camera data is used only to create PDF files stored locally on your device. No images or video are transmitted externally.

Document Storage

All scanned documents are saved in the app's private storage on your device. Documents are not uploaded to any cloud service or server. You can delete documents at any time from within the app.

OCR (Text Recognition)

Text extraction from scanned documents is performed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No document content is sent to external services for processing.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Analytics

ScanlyPDF does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

ScanlyPDF does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
