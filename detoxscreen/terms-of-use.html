DetoxScreen is a digital wellbeing app for iPhone that helps you create scheduled routines to restrict access to selected apps. The app uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to automatically apply and remove app restrictions based on your configured schedules.

6. App Restrictions

When a routine is active, DetoxScreen uses Apple's ManagedSettings framework to shield selected apps. Please note:

  • Restrictions are applied and removed automatically based on your schedule
  • Each routine uses its own isolated settings store, so multiple routines do not interfere with each other
  • You can disable any routine at any time from the app to immediately remove its restrictions
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all restrictions
  • Emergency and essential system apps are never restricted by Apple's framework
